About India

Geography: India is located in South Asia, bordered by Pakistan, China, Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh, and Myanmar. It has a diverse landscape, including the Himalayas, the Thar Desert, and the Deccan Plateau.

History: India is home to one of the world's oldest civilizations, the Indus Valley Civilization. It gained independence from British rule on August 15, 1947.

Culture: India is a multilingual and multicultural country with 22 officially recognized languages. It is the birthplace of Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism, and hosts a variety of festivals like Diwali, Holi, and Eid.

Economy: India has one of the world's largest economies, with a strong focus on technology, services, and agriculture. Cities like Bangalore and Hyderabad are global tech hubs.

Challenges: Despite its growth, India faces issues like poverty, healthcare disparities, and environmental concerns such as pollution and water scarcity.
