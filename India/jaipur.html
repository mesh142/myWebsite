Jaipur - The Pink City

Culture: Known for its royal heritage and vibrant festivals.

Famous For: Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, and Jaipur Literature Festival.

Food: Dal Baati Churma, Ghewar, and Laal Maas.

Clothing: Traditional Rajasthani attire like Bandhani sarees and Lehengas.
